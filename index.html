About

Hey there, I am a game designer. I have been working professionally as a game designer and game developer for over two years now, along with a small team of developers and 3D artists. I've worked on games in a variety of genres, including horror, puzzle, 2D platformers, shooters, and action-adventure. I also have experience designing and developing games for PC and VR. I am open to working on different game genres and novel ideas, as I am always looking for a new challenge in terms of game design.

Sarcophagus


A tower defense-esque shooter game made for virtual reality devices like HTC VIVE and HTC Cosmos using Unity and SteamVR.

Things I worked on

  1. Designing the level layout.
  2. Developing different weapons like a handgun, an SMG, a machete and a hand grenade.
  3. Scripting snap zones that aact as holsters that allow the player to holster and switch guns in VR.
  4. Developing Enemy AI that has different states like chase, attack and dodge.
  5. Developing a system that calculates and keeps track of players score.

The main goal of the game is to survive as long as possible with the weapons in your arsenal while earning the highest score. Players get to experience features like shooting weapons, throwing grenades, manually reloading guns, and holstering or switching out weapons in VR.
