Projects


Crimson Spire


A 3D action adventure game in it's early stages. The player plays the role of a travelling warrior on a journey to the Crimson Spire.

The player will find themselves near a small village when the game starts. Interact with the NPCs to get to know about the village and acquire new quests that will help you progress through the game. Experience day and night cycle and encounter different enemy types throughout your journey.

This is a short demo of the game and is made using new Unity Input system due to which it can be played using mouse and keyboard or a controller.

Try the game for yourself.



Silverwood Hospital


Silverwood Hospital is a short 3d horror/puzzle game. The player assumes the role of a small time reporter trying to make their way around a dark and abandoned hospital in order to gather evidence to check if the rumors about the shady experiments conducted in the hospital are true.

The player encounters various puzzles which need to be solved in order to progress through the game and collect evidence tapes. The ending of the game is influenced by the number of tapes collected during the playthrough. There is total of 5 tapes that can be collected and 3 different endings that can be achieved.

Try the game for yourself.



Sarcophagus


A tower defense-esque shooter game made for Virtual Reality devices like HTC VIVE and HTC Cosmos using Unity and SteamVR

The player is placed in the middle of the level right next to a sarcophagus with the aim to stop the waves of mummies approaching from each direction. The player is equipped with multiple weapons like a pistol, tommy gun for long range combat, a machete for close combat and a stick grenade to get out of a sticky situation.

The main aim of the game is to try and survive for as long as possible with the weapons in your arsenal and get the highest score. Players get to experience features like shooting weapons, lobbing grenades and manually reloading guns and holstering/switching out weapons in VR.




Serial - The escape room


A virutal reality escape room horror experience with large amount of challenging and interactive puzzles.

The players will find themselves trapped in the basement of a serial killer with nothing to their name. Familiarize yourself with the environment as you interact with various objects and come across clues to solve the puzzles that will help you escape.

Getting out of the basement is only the beginning as the player will then need to navigate through the entire house to solve even more challenging yet interesting puzzles, and in turn get to know more about the dreaded place that they are trapped in.
