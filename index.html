About


Hello! I am Jay Alaknure, a passionate game developer. I live in Mumbai, India. I graduated in the year 2019 and got my bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering. I have been working on making video games which includes creating assets, designing levels, writing code, making prototypes etc for about a year now.

When it comes to games no genre is off limits for me, I always like to explore new concepts. So, if you have any kind of ideas, be it for a full fledged game or a one-off Game jam feel free to contact me.
